# UX, Art, and Audio Playbook

Use this role set when work affects moment-to-moment feel, camera, input, UI,
level readability, visual hierarchy, animation, VFX, sound, music, or ambience.

## Review Questions

- What should the player do now?
- What tells the player that action is possible?
- What confirms success, failure, danger, or partial progress?
- Does the camera support the action without stealing control?
- Can the player separate objective, threat, interactable, and background?
- Which sound has gameplay priority?
- What happens after a mistake?
- Which accessibility setting or fallback is needed for this moment?

## Blocking Concerns

- The player cannot read the immediate goal.
- The control loop feels delayed, unstable, or unconfirmed.
- Visual polish competes with gameplay hierarchy.
- Audio masks critical feedback.
- Camera motion breaks trust or comfort.
- A level tests a rule before teaching it.
- Accessibility barriers are built into the core interaction.
