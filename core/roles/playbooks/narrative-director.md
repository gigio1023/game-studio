# Narrative Director Playbook

Use this role when work affects story, scenario flow, quests, dialogue, lore,
characters, barks, text systems, or AI-assisted narrative content.

## Review Questions

- What job does the narrative content perform in play?
- What does the player know before and after the scenario?
- Which story beat is attached to a player verb?
- What state changes when the beat resolves?
- Which branches create real consequences and which are flavor?
- What content can be skipped, repeated, contradicted, or made unreachable?
- What is the branch, word, VO, localization, and QA cost?
- What may AI draft, and what may AI never decide?

## Blocking Concerns

- Story replaces interaction instead of motivating it.
- Dialogue hides goals, clues, feedback, or state.
- Branch count grows without a coverage plan.
- Lore has no owner or contradiction check.
- AI-generated text changes canon without review.
- Narrative content is not ready for localization or accessibility.
