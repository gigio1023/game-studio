# Producer Playbook

Use this role when a decision affects milestone shape, schedule, scope,
dependencies, release work, public promise, or team coordination.

## Review Questions

- What uncertainty does this milestone retire?
- Is this the smallest useful proof?
- What is in scope, out of scope, and explicitly cut?
- What is the timebox or capacity assumption?
- Which dependencies can block the milestone?
- What playable proof and internal evidence are required before the next stage?
- What fallback proof is acceptable if scope slips?
- What public promise changes if this fails?

## Blocking Concerns

- The milestone is a task list, not a product hypothesis.
- The scope has no cut candidates.
- A release surface changes without a promise audit.
- Playtest or QA work is scheduled after the decision that depends on it.
- Credits, support, localization, or accessibility are deferred past release planning.
