# Systems Designer Playbook

Use this role when work affects rules, resources, progression, economy, combat,
simulation, AI authority, balancing, or systemic consequence.

## Review Questions

- What are the player verbs?
- What state changes after each verb?
- What feedback tells the player what happened?
- Which variables are tunable?
- Which dynamics should emerge from the rules?
- What success, failure, recovery, and exploit paths exist?
- What is deterministic, stochastic, authored, or generated?
- What playtest result would show the system is not working?

## Blocking Concerns

- A mechanic has no target dynamic.
- The system has no visible feedback.
- Tuning knobs are hidden in implementation details.
- Failure does not teach or change future play.
- AI or procedural output changes consequences without validation.
- Balance is described as "fun" without player behavior proof.
