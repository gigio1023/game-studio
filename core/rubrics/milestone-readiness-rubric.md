# Milestone Readiness Rubric

## READY

- Goal is specific.
- Non-goals are explicit.
- Required evidence is listed.
- Verification commands or manual checks are listed.
- Review roles are named.
- Exit decision is defined.

## CONCERNS

- The milestone can start, but contains risks or unclear boundaries.

## NOT_READY

- The milestone is a task pile.
- The evidence is subjective.
- The scope is unbounded.
- The milestone claims a stage it cannot prove.
