# Milestone Readiness Rubric

## READY

- Goal is specific.
- Direction source and selected concept are named when relevant.
- Proof target is explicit.
- Proof budget is bounded.
- Non-goals are explicit.
- Required internal evidence artifacts are listed.
- Verification commands or manual checks are listed.
- Review roles are named.
- Required review gates are named.
- Exit decision is defined.
- Smallest useful proof is named.
- Cut candidates and fallback proof are named.
- Playable proof is required when the milestone makes a player-facing claim.

## CONCERNS

- The milestone can start, but contains risks or unclear boundaries.

## NOT_READY

- The milestone is a task pile.
- The proof target is subjective or vague.
- The scope is unbounded.
- The milestone claims a stage it cannot prove.
- The milestone depends on unreviewed accessibility, narrative, localization, release, or QA risk.
