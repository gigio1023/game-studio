# Release Review

Use this before changing public pages, trailers, screenshots, demos, builds,
credits, localization claims, accessibility claims, or support promises.

## Questions

- What public claim is being made?
- Which build or capture proves it?
- What known limitation must be disclosed?
- Are credits, licenses, support, localization, and accessibility claims true?
- What store or demo area must change if the proof is weak?
- What should be removed rather than promised?

## Blocking Concerns

- Store copy promises unsupported features.
- Trailer or screenshots show behavior absent from the build.
- Accessibility, localization, or controller support is overstated.
- Credits or asset licenses are missing.
- Support expectations are unclear.

## Output

```text
Release claim:
Build truth:
Proof checked:
Internal evidence checked:
Claim change required:
Required next proof:
```
