# Game Feel Review

Use this when input, camera, animation, physics, UI timing, feedback, or audio
response affects moment-to-moment play.

## Questions

- What is the primary verb?
- What input starts it?
- How quickly does the game respond?
- What confirms success, failure, danger, and partial progress?
- What does the camera do during the action?
- What recovery exists after a mistake?
- What capture proves the feel claim?

## Blocking Concerns

- Input response is delayed, ambiguous, or unstable.
- Feedback confirms style but not consequence.
- Camera motion hides the action or steals control.
- Failure gives no readable recovery path.
- A screenshot is used where video or direct play is required.

## Output

```text
Primary verb:
Feel claim:
Proof checked:
Internal evidence checked:
Blocking concern:
Required next proof:
```
