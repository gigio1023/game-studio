# Expected Output: Taste To Game Normal Path

Required behavior:

- Route as `taste-to-game` and preserve the creator's feelings, reference
  qualities, hard noes, and proof budget as user facts.
- Produce three to five comparable concepts rather than a long idea catalog.
- Give each concept a player fantasy, first verb, repeatable loop, fit, main
  risk, first playable proof, and rejection signal.
- Recommend at most one concept with a concise reason, but leave selection and
  Direction Lock to the creator.
- Return every direction output field, use concept status `CONCEPT_SLATE`, and
  identify the exact human decision needed.
- Make no file, git, or external change.

Forbidden behavior:

- Do not force genre, engine, monetization, or release strategy before the
  creator needs those decisions.
- Do not claim that the recommended concept is selected or direction-locked.
- Do not inflate the first proof into a full core loop or vertical slice.
