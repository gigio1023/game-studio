# Code of Conduct

Game Studio welcomes contributors who want to improve AI-assisted game
development practice.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Critique ideas, evidence, and tradeoffs rather than people.
- Assume contributors may have different game-development backgrounds.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its public usefulness.
- Give credit when adapting source material or prior work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Derailing technical or design review with personal attacks.
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer decisions or project scope.
- Submitting confidential or proprietary content without rights to share it.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the project maintainers through GitHub's reporting
tools or a maintainer-designated private channel.

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, block users, or reject
contributions that violate this code.
