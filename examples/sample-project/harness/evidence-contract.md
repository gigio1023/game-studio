# Evidence Contract Example

## Milestone

M1 Protocol Proof

## Evidence Required

| Evidence | Owner | Path or URL | Pass Criteria |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Runtime evidence | Technical Director | `evidence/m1_protocol_proof.json` | Valid and invalid actions produce distinct outcomes. |
| Playable capture | QA Lead | `evidence/screenshots/m1_protocol_proof.png` | The rule response and visible consequence are clear. |

## Rejection Criteria

- The consequence is only described in prose.
- The player cannot repeat the action.
- The proof depends on undocumented manual setup.
